Today was packed — I had two completely different adventures back-to-back. First, I went to **Mind Code** for coding class, and the teacher was super nice. He told us our next project is going to be **Rock, Paper, Scissors**, and I can't wait to get started. Coding in Python is really fun, especially because we get to use special commands that feel almost like magic.

Today, I learned about **if statements** and also about order — meaning the order your code runs in. I figured out that when you write an if statement, you have to put a tab in front of the `print` line (or anything else you want the if statement to control). Otherwise, Python won't know that line belongs inside the if. It took me a minute to get the hang of it, but once I did, it clicked.

The way I think of it, an **if statement is like a little box**. You can only get inside the box if the condition is **True**. If it's **False**, the door stays shut and you can't access what's inside. That picture really helps me remember how it works.

After I wrapped up coding, I headed over to **Softland Studio**, and wow — there were tons of people there. I walked up and told them I could lend a hand, and they put me right to work as a **part-time helper**. My job was to assist the customers with all kinds of handcraft projects — some people were doing **stained glass soldering**, others were polishing the edges of glass pieces, and a few were working on rugs. I got to run around helping wherever I was needed, and it felt awesome to actually be useful.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6307.jpeg)

The manager was so happy with my work that at the end of the day, he handed me **$100 in cash**. I couldn't believe it — that was the first time I ever earned real money from a job! I felt really proud holding those bills in my hand.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6312.jpeg)

It was such a fun day, and afterward I had dinner — I was starving by then! I really, really hope I get to go back and help out again soon. Today was one of those days that just flew by because I was having so much fun.
