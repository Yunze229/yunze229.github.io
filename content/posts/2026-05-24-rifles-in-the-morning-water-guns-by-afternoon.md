  玩完这一切，我们就回家了，我弟弟湿淋淋地坐在车里——其实还挺好笑的。不过一回到家，就有点无聊了。我真希望能再去 Ethan 家，因为我们之所以得提前走，就是因为我弟弟被浇湿了。也许下次我能多待一会儿。
---

This morning, we headed to the shooting range to practice with our air rifles. We went there to train alongside the high-level shooters, and honestly, getting to shoot next to them always pushes me to do my best. It's one of my favorite ways to start the day.

After that, we stopped at a charging station to charge my mom's car, because the battery was running low. Once it was all charged up, we went and bought some snacks, and then we shared them with our friends — it felt nice to spread a little kindness around.

Then I got to hang out with my friend Ethan, and that's when the real fun began. We played water balloon toss and went all out with the water guns. It turned into a full-on water fight! My little brother ended up getting completely soaked, but somehow I managed to stay dry the whole time.

After all that, we headed home with my brother sitting there dripping wet in the car — it was pretty funny, actually. Once we got home, though, things got kind of boring. I really wish I could go back to Ethan's house, because the only reason we had to leave was that my brother got soaked. Maybe next time I'll get to stay longer.
