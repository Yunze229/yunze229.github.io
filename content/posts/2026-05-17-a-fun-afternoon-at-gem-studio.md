Today I went to a place called Gem Studio. I brought my own stones, hoping to use them, but I was disappointed to find out they weren’t accepting customers’ stones yet. Even so, I still had a lot of fun helping Mom make her bracelet.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6252.jpeg)

It turned out you could make many different things with the beads there, but I chose a bracelet because the staff said it was the most popular. They let us pick our own stones, and there were also tiny diamond decorations you could use to spell out your name. Unfortunately, I didn’t leave enough space on mine, so I couldn’t really use that part — but it was still really fun.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6262.jpeg)

The trickiest step was opening the little rings and connecting them to the bracelet by yourself. You have to use small pliers, and it takes a bit of practice to get it right. Once I got the hang of it, it actually felt pretty satisfying.

One important thing to watch out for is bending the rings. If you’re not careful, they can get sharp or even break, and then they might fall on the floor where you’d never find them. Despite that small challenge, I really enjoyed the whole experience.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6276.jpeg)

If I get the chance, I’d definitely love to go back.
