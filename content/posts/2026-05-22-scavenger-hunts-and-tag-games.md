Today, I went on an exciting **field trip**. It was incredibly fun but quite tiring! Even though it was only a 10-minute ride, we still got to take the school bus to get there.

As soon as we arrived, we spent 20 minutes playing and having snacks. Next, we were given a whole hour to visit different stations and go on a scavenger hunt. I was so excited because we found everything on our list! Afterward, we gathered back at the welcome station and received prizes.

My favorite part was the free play time in the park. I had a blast playing tag with my friends. Afterward, I was so exhausted that I just rested under the nice, cool **shade of a tree**.

When we returned to school, our teacher gave us a choice: "If you want to stay inside, stay inside; if you want to go outside, go outside." I decided to stay indoors because I was completely worn out. Once I finished my homework, it was finally time to go home. What a wonderful and unforgettable day!
