Hi, I'm Yunze.

I was born on February 29, 2016 — a leap year birthday. That means I only get a real birthday once every four years. While most people my age have had around ten birthdays, I've had about two and a half. I think that's a good reminder: time is strange, and how you spend it matters more than how much of it you have.

I believe in the long game. Beautiful things take time — learning, understanding, growing. I'm not in a hurry.

I like math because it's honest. I like science because it asks good questions. I like debate because it forces you to think from the other side.

I also want to see the world.

This is where I write things down — what I'm learning, where I've been, what I'm thinking about. Some posts are carefully written. Some are just notes. Both count.
